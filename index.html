Frequently asked questions

Is this just about drinking?

No. The focus is on social contact. Many people choose beer, but soft drinks, tea or coffee are just as welcome. Come for the company, not the alcohol.

Who can come?

Pub Club is aimed at men who want more social contact, but local groups can decide how inclusive they want to be. The aim is to be welcoming, relaxed and kind.

Does it cost anything?

There‚Äôs no membership fee. You just pay for your own drink(s) and maybe a snack if you want one.

No. Pub Club is not professional support ‚Äì it‚Äôs simply friendly social contact. If someone needs more help, encourage them to speak to a GP or relevant service.
